What is PaidPunch?
PaidPunch is a fun new way for customers to save money and win prizes at businesses throughout their city. It creates a unique win-win scenario where you, the customer, get a great discount and the business gets exposure to new customers as well as repeat business. The key to PaidPunch is this cool thing we've created called a "Punch".

What is a “Punch”?
A Punch is a digital voucher of store credit. Businesses sell them in packs called PaidPunch cards. Punches are used towards your purchase at the business you purchased it from.

What is a “Mystery Punch”?
A Mystery Punch is similar to a Punch in the sense that it has value and is redeemable at the business you bought it from. However, unlike a regular Punch which has a known value, the Mystery Punch has a random value that can be anything from a free drink to $20 cash! It adds a great twist to using PaidPunch that is fun, rewarding, social and quite addicting!

Give me a scenario.
Okay, sure. Starkey's BBQ sells 4 $1.75 Punches (a $7 value) for just $2. Plus, they also give you one FREE Mystery Punch which can be anything from a free drink to a free meal. You can even win $20 cash!

So, for your $2 investment, you can get up to $27 worth of value! Talk about a great deal!

Do I have to spend money to use PaidPunch?
Not at all. Businesses offer free Punches which you can unlock by sharing a post to your Facebook! Then, if you like the business you can take 26 seconds to enter in your credit card information and buy Punches. It's up to you.

Is my credit card information kept safe and secure?
Absolutely. Keeping your credit card information secure is our top priority. Without your trust, we cannot operate. That is why we store all of your data in a highly secure offsite location with the industry leader in credit card processing, Authorize.net. All sensitive information is sent across a secure 256-bit encrypted connection.

How did PaidPunch start? What's the story?
PaidPunch was started by two University of San Diego students on a mission to create a service that gives great deals to consumers while generating profitable repeat business for businesses.
